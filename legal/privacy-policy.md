# Privacy Policy

**AllNightLong ("ANL")**
**Last updated: March 23, 2026**
**Effective date: March 23, 2026**

---

## 1. Introduction

AllNightLong ("ANL," "we," "us," or "our") operates the AllNightLong mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using ANL, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

- **Account information**: Name, email address, date of birth, gender identity, and sexual orientation (optional)
- **Profile information**: Photos, biography, interests, and preferences
- **Communications**: Messages sent through the App, including text and media
- **Payment information**: Processed securely by Stripe; we do not store full card numbers

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Location data**: Precise GPS location when you use the App, used to show nearby matches
- **Device information**: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, push notification tokens
- **Usage data**: Features used, swipe activity, session duration, app interactions
- **Log data**: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time and date of visits

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties

- **Authentication providers**: If you sign in via Apple or Google
- **Analytics providers**: Aggregated, anonymized usage data

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Create and manage your account
- Show you potential matches based on your preferences and location
- Facilitate messaging and video calls between matched users
- Process subscription payments and manage billing
- Send push notifications for matches, messages, and calls
- Moderate content and enforce our Community Guidelines
- Improve the App through analytics and AI-driven features
- Comply with legal obligations
- Prevent fraud, abuse, and safety violations

### 3.1 AI Features

ANL uses AI to provide conversation coaching, compatibility scoring, and match recommendations. This processing occurs on our servers. Your data is not shared with third-party AI providers for training purposes.

---

## 4. Location Data

ANL uses your location to:

- Display your distance to other users (shown as approximate, e.g., "2 miles away")
- Power the NightPulse activity map feature
- Improve match relevance

You can disable location access in your device settings. Disabling location will limit discovery features but will not prevent use of the App entirely.

We do not sell your precise location data to third parties.

---

## 5. Photos and Media

Photos you upload are:

- Stored on Amazon S3 with access controls
- Scanned automatically for prohibited content (nudity, violence) using moderation software
- Visible only to users you match with or, for profile photos, users in your discovery area

We retain photos for 30 days after account deletion before permanent removal.

---

## 6. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in these circumstances:

- **With other users**: Profile information and messages as part of normal App functionality
- **Service providers**: AWS (storage), Stripe (payments), Expo (push notifications), Railway (hosting) — bound by data processing agreements
- **Legal requirements**: When required by law, subpoena, or to protect rights and safety
- **Business transfers**: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with notice to you

---

## 7. Data Retention

| Data type | Retention period |
|---|---|
| Account data | Duration of account + 30 days after deletion |
| Messages | 1 year, or deleted on request |
| Location history | 7 days rolling |
| Payment records | 7 years (legal requirement) |

## 8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your account and associated data
- **Portability**: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
- **Correction**: Update inaccurate information
- **Opt-out**: Opt out of certain data processing activities

To exercise these rights, contact us at **privacy@anl.app** or use the data controls in Settings → Privacy.

**California residents (CCPA)**: You have the right to know, delete, and opt out of the sale of personal information. ANL does not sell personal information.

**EU/UK residents (GDPR)**: Our legal bases for processing are consent, contract performance, and legitimate interests. You may lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

---

## 9. Children's Privacy

ANL is not directed to persons under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has created an account, contact us at **safety@anl.app** and we will promptly remove the account.

## 10. Security

We implement industry-standard safeguards:

- TLS encryption for data in transit
- AES-256 encryption for sensitive data at rest
- JWT tokens with short expiry for authentication
- Regular security audits
- Employee access controls and NDAs

No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
